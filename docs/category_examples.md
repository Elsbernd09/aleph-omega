# Categorical Examples

## Purpose

Phase 12 gives Project ℵω a category-like structural layer.

Objects are finite logical universes.

Morphisms are finite bridges.

Identity morphisms are identity bridges.

Composition is bridge composition.

The goal is not to claim that the project has developed a full new category theory. The goal is to show that the finite bridge system obeys the basic structural laws expected from a category-like framework.

---

## Example 1: Starter Finite Universe Category

The starter category contains:

- finite classical universe
- finite modal universe
- identity bridge on the classical universe
- identity bridge on the modal universe
- modal-to-classical collapse bridge

This example shows that a bridge can be structurally well behaved even if it semantically distorts modal features.

---

## Example 2: Identity-Only Category

The simplest example has:

- one finite classical universe
- one identity bridge

This satisfies identity and associativity in the most basic possible way.

---

## Example 3: Collapse Structure

A modal-to-classical collapse bridge maps statements from a modal source into a classical target.

Structurally, this bridge can satisfy identity laws and associativity.
